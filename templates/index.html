Music similarity web survey

In this survey, you will be presented three tracks from your song library.
Listen to each of them, and then select the odd one out, i.e. the one that feels the less similar to the others. This will be used to then train bliss to know what kind of playlists you like, by performing metric learning.
Make sure that you are in an environment where you can listen to music properly and comfortably.

All your answers are saved and stored in blissify's local database every time you submit.
